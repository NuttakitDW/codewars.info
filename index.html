How it works

Join in the fun final event of the year before 2020 ends. Get into the CodeWar fun! Tis' the season to be coding. It's not limited to any coding language. Complete the task correctly and fast to get into the next round. Form a team of 3-5 people with your friends or simply turn up and join forces with other code wizards in the crowd! Unsure? Join the the fun by bringing your laptop and try to beat the official teams from the audience. Complete the fun instant knockout tournament to seize the cup and bring the glory home as the winning team. May the best team win.
